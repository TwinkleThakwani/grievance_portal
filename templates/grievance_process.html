Grievance Process

Understand the steps involved in resolving grievances.

Steps to Submit a Grievance

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Navigate to the "Submit Grievance" section.
  3. Fill out the grievance form with all required details.
  4. Attach any supporting evidence, if applicable.
  5. Submit the grievance and track its status in your dashboard.

Resolution Process

Once submitted, your grievance will be assigned to an investigator who will gather evidence, analyze the issue, and provide a resolution.
