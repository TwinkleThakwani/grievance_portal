Anti-Ragging

Committed to a Safe, Supportive, and Inclusive Campus Environment

What is Ragging?

Ragging is any behavior that causes physical or emotional harm, or creates a hostile environment for a student. This includes:

  • Physical abuse or assault
  • Verbal abuse or harassment
  • Forced participation in activities
  • Exploitation or intimidation

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Preventing and addressing ragging incidents
  • Conducting workshops and awareness programs
  • Providing counseling and support to victims
  • Investigating and taking action against perpetrators

Consequences of Ragging

  • Suspension or expulsion from college
  • Fines or penalties
  • Criminal charges

Procedure for Reporting Ragging

If you are a victim of ragging or witness an incident, please report it immediately to any member of the ARC.
