Student Grievance Redressal Committee (GRC)

Ensuring Fairness, Transparency, and Support for All

At Lachoo Memorial College of Science and Technology we are committed to providing a fair, transparent, and supportive environment for all students, faculty, and staff. The Grievance Redressal Committee (GRC) is established to address and resolve grievances in a timely and effective manner.


Purpose

The purpose of the GRC is to provide a platform for students, faculty, and staff to report their grievances and concerns, and to ensure that these are addressed in a fair, impartial, and confidential manner.


Scope

The GRC will address grievances related to:

  • Academic matters
  • Administrative issues
  • Infrastructure and facilities
  • Harassment and discrimination
  • Other concerns affecting the college community

Confidentiality

All grievances will be treated confidentially, and the identity of the complainant will be protected.
