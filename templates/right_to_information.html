Right to Information

Learn about your rights under the Right to Information Act.

What is RTI?

The Right to Information Act, 2005 empowers citizens to seek information from public authorities, promoting transparency and accountability.

How to File an RTI?

  • Submit a written application to the designated Public Information Officer (PIO).
  • Provide clear details about the information you seek.
  • Pay the prescribed fee as per the RTI rules.

Contact for RTI

For RTI-related queries, contact the college's Public Information Officer at office@lachoomemorial.org.
