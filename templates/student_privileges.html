Student Privileges

Learn about the rights and privileges available to students at Lachoo Memorial College of Science & Technology.

Academic Privileges

  • Access to library resources, digital databases, and study materials.
  • Opportunities for academic counseling, mentorship, and remedial classes.
  • Participation in seminars, workshops, and conferences.
  • Eligibility for scholarships, awards, and academic recognition.
  • Right to fair and transparent evaluation and re-evaluation of answer scripts.

Campus Facilities

  • Use of laboratories, computer centers, and research facilities.
  • Access to sports, gymnasium, and recreational amenities.
  • Participation in cultural, technical, and social events.
  • Safe and inclusive hostel accommodation (where applicable).
  • Medical and counseling support services.

Grievance Redressal

  • Right to submit grievances and receive timely, impartial resolutions.
  • Access to a designated grievance redressal officer or committee.
  • Protection against retaliation for filing grievances.
