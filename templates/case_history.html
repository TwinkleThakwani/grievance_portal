Iconic Case: Library Access Issue

In 2024, a group of students raised a grievance about limited library access hours, which affected their ability to prepare for exams. The grievance system quickly assigned an investigator to the case. After gathering feedback from students and faculty, the system facilitated a resolution by extending library hours during exam periods. This change benefited over 1,000 students and improved academic performance across the college.

Resolution: Extended library hours during exams.

How the System Helps

  • Students submit grievances online through a user-friendly interface.
  • Grievances are automatically assigned to investigators based on priority.
  • Investigators gather evidence and submit reports.
  • Resolutions are implemented promptly, ensuring student satisfaction.
  • Feedback is collected to improve the system continuously.
