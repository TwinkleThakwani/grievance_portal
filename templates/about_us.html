About College

Lachoo Memorial College of Science & Technology, a modest creation of the year 1965, has travelled an incredible journey of more than five decades. For concepts savoured by visionary Shri Mathuradas Mathur, a connoisseur of higher education in science and technology, this institution has come up synonymous with impeccable academic standards preferred for pursuing multi-disciplinary graduate and post-graduate programmes in the field of science, computers, pharmacy, bio-technology, and management.

Boasting with its imposing presence, the college spans over a 7-acre land located in the heart of the city yet with a welcome departure from the usual hustle and bustle of the city. The well-qualified, devoted, committed, and excellent faculty of the college has raised the academic ambience upholding quality assurance of unfaltering education excellence in the college.

About 1500 young learners annually come here from the entire country as their choicest heaven, making this college today as the shelter of quality benchmark in higher education.

The College is recognized under section 2(F) and 12(B) in the UGC act and affiliated to Jai Narain Vyas University, Jodhpur. The College has the unique distinction of being the first and only College in Western Rajasthan which has been awarded 'A' grade by NAAC and selected as "College with Potential for Excellence" by UGC and selected under the Star College Scheme by the Department of Biotechnology, Govt of India. The college has now heralded emerging as the foremost 'Autonomous College', commencing from the academic session 2012-13.



Vision

We, at Lachoo Memorial College of Science & Technology envisage building a progressive learning community with scientific aptitude. We set global standards to make our students scientifically and ethically stronger. They, in turn, will serve the nation and human society to improve the quality of life.

Mission

Our Mission is to Provide High Quality Value-based education to younger generation through a synthesis of science & technology and mould their character that their earnest endeavour to achieve progress and prosperity in life is matched by an ardent desire to extend selfless service to the society, one complementing the other.

Inspiration

Late Sh. Mathuradas Mathur 'Bha' Ji
Late Sh. Man Mohan Mathur 'Lachoo' Ji

Lachoo Memorial College of Science & Technology (Autonomous), Jodhpur, a pioneer in advancing higher education in science and technology was established in the fond memory of Late Sh. Man Mohan Mathur ‘Lachoo’ Ji by his elder brother Late Sh. Mathuradas Mathur ‘Bha’ Ji after untimely demise at the age of 40 (1921-1961).

‘Lachoo Ji’ was a freedom fighter, a prominent leader in the Quit India Movement, and a steadfast follower of Gandhian ideals. He played a pivotal role in the freedom struggle of Marwar.

Today, the college embodies the values of determination, resilience and service to society. The institution continues to inspire generations, blending its rich historical roots with a modern vision for education and innovation.



About the Grievance Redressal System

The Grievance Redressal System at Lachoo Memorial College of Science & Technology is designed to ensure that every student's voice is heard and addressed promptly. The system provides a structured mechanism for students to report grievances related to academics, campus facilities, or any other concerns.

Key Features:

  • Submit grievances online through a user-friendly interface.
  • Track the status of submitted grievances in real-time.
  • Assigned investigators ensure timely resolution of grievances.
  • Feedback mechanism to ensure continuous improvement.

Our goal is to create a transparent and efficient system that fosters trust and ensures a positive campus experience for all students.
