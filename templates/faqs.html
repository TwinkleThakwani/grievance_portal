Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the grievance system.

General Questions

  • Q: How do I submit a grievance?
    A: Log in to your account and fill out the grievance submission form.
  • Q: Can I track the status of my grievance?
    A: Yes, you can track the status in your dashboard.
  • Q: Who can access my grievance details?
    A: Only authorized investigators and administrators.
