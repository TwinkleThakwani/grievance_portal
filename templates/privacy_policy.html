Privacy Policy

Understand how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Introduction

Welcome to the Grievance Management System of Lachoo Memorial College of Science & Technology. By using this website, you agree to abide by the terms and conditions outlined in this Privacy Policy. This policy governs the collection, use, and protection of your personal information while using the grievance system.

Information Collection and Use

  • Personal Information: We collect your name, email address, student ID, and contact details when you register or submit a grievance. This information is used solely for addressing and resolving grievances.
  • Grievance Details: Information related to grievances, including descriptions, evidence, and respondent details, is collected to investigate and resolve grievances effectively.
  • Cookies and Analytics: We may use cookies and third-party tools like Google Analytics to enhance user experience and analyze website traffic.

Data Usage

  • Purpose: The collected data is used to process grievances, assign investigators, and provide updates on grievance status.
  • Sharing of Information: Personal information may be shared with authorized investigators and administrators involved in resolving grievances. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Public Records: Certain grievance details may become part of public records, subject to disclosure under relevant regulations.

Data Security

  • Protection Measures: We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.
  • User Responsibility: Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials and for any activity conducted under their accounts.

Website Usage

  • Content Ownership: All content on this website, including text, images, and graphics, is the property of Lachoo Memorial College of Science & Technology and is protected by copyright laws.
  • Website Availability: We strive to keep the website operational at all times. However, we are not responsible for temporary unavailability due to technical issues beyond our control.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

Lachoo Memorial College of Science & Technology reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice. Users are encouraged to review the policy periodically to stay informed about any updates.

Disclaimer

  • Limitation of Liability: We are not liable for any loss or damage arising from the use of this website or reliance on its content.
